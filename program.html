✦ 40 days of transformation

Beyond Limits — the 40-day program

Day by day. Step by step. Safely guided, yet deeply yours. A program that doesn't teach you who to be — it helps you remember who you already are.

What it is

Deep practice in a simple form

40 days. Each day brings a short guided text, meditation or practice, a reflection question, and space for your own experience. The program is designed to be gentle enough for a busy life — and deep enough for real transformation.

I combine bhakti (devotion), meditation, journaling, and gentle work with the body. No dogma. Just tools that have worked for me on my own journey — and that I now share with you.

This journey is for you if…

02

What one day looks like

i

Opening thought

A short text to carry into your day.

ii

Guided practice

Meditation, breath or gentle movement — 10–20 min.

iii

Reflection

One honest question, not for the mind only.

iv

Journaling

Space for your own words. No right answer.

v

Affirmation

One sentence to hold the rest of your day.

What shifts in 40 days

From chaos → to calm

A gradual settling of the mind that has learned to always be in motion. The nervous system remembers how to rest.

From rejection → to acceptance

Working with shame and the inner critic. A softening that comes from the inside out.

From isolation → to connection

Feeling your body, other people, and yourself again. Without walls, without constant self-protection.

From identity → to being

Moving beyond "who am I" toward simply being — the ground that was always there.
