Welcome to Beyond Limits

A new home for my writings, services, and the 40-day journey. Thank you for being here.

Hello, beautiful soul. My name is Romana — Anuradha. I'm so happy you're reading this.

For a long time my work has lived in different places — on my blog at anuradha.blog, on Instagram, in conversations, in retreats. This site, Beyond Limits, is my attempt to bring it all together — to give you (and me) one clear home where everything makes sense.

Why "Beyond Limits"

The name carries the whole story. Beyond the limits we were given as children. Beyond the limits society places on identity, on gender, on who is allowed to seek the divine. Beyond the limits we place on ourselves out of fear.

And also — beyond the limits of what we thought was possible. The 40-day journey is built around exactly this: not to fix you, but to remind you that the limits you've been living with were never the truth.

"Love is not what you want, but who you are."

What you'll find here

  • Services — meditations, consultations, and spiritual travel guidance, all in one place
  • The 40-day program — Beyond Limits, the inner transformation journey
  • About — my full story, including my work as a teacher's assistant and my e-book
  • Pricing — clear, transparent, no surprises
  • Blog — this space, where I'll share new reflections as they arise

My older writings live on at anuradha.blog — I'll keep posting there too. This blog will hold my newest thoughts as Beyond Limits grows.

Walk with me

Whatever brought you here — curiosity, longing, a hard moment, a beautiful one — I'm grateful you arrived. If anything calls to you, write to me. We'll find the right next step together.

With love,
Romana Anuradha 🍀🌞❤️
